Moon Knight

About Moon Knight

He is the alter ego of Marc Spector , a CIA agent of Israeli origin, who died during a mission in Egypt, but returned to life thanks to a pact with the God Khonshu. He appears to him in a vision, offering him a second chance at life if he becomes the god's avatar on earth.
